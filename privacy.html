Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Archie ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses and protects your personal data when you use our AI life coaching service at coach-blueprint.netlify.app.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Who We Are

Archie is an independent, self-funded AI life coaching web application. For data protection enquiries, contact us at: hello@archie.app

2. What Data We Collect

When you use Archie, we collect the following information:

  • Account data — your name, email address and encrypted password when you create an account
  • Weekly plan data — your calendar events, goals, and weekly commitments that you share with Archie
  • Feature plan data — your travel, running, nutrition and workout preferences and generated plans
  • Usage data — how you interact with the app, which features you use, and when you log in
  • Device data — browser type, device type, and IP address collected automatically via analytics

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the Archie service:

  • To create and manage your account
  • To generate personalised AI plans and recommendations
  • To save and display your weekly dashboard and feature plans
  • To track your goal progress and streaks across sessions
  • To improve the app based on how people use it
  • To send transactional emails (password resets only — no marketing emails without consent)

4. AI Processing

Archie uses Anthropic's Claude AI to generate your personalised plans and respond to your messages. When you chat with Archie, your messages are sent to Anthropic's API for processing. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to this processing and can be found at anthropic.com/privacy.

We do not use your personal conversations to train AI models. Your chat history is not permanently stored on Anthropic's servers.

5. Data Storage

Your account and plan data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider. Data is stored in the EU and protected by row-level security — meaning only you can access your own data. Supabase's privacy policy can be found at supabase.com/privacy.

6. Cookies and Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how people use Archie. This uses cookies to collect anonymised usage data including pages visited, time spent, and general location (country level). No personally identifiable information is shared with Google Analytics.

We ask for your consent before activating analytics tracking. You can withdraw consent at any time by declining cookies.

We do not use advertising cookies or sell your data to advertisers.

7. Advertising

Archie displays advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to show relevant ads based on your browsing history. You can opt out of personalised advertising at adssettings.google.com.

8. Affiliate Links

Archie includes affiliate links to third-party products and services (including Nike, Gymshark, Skyscanner, Hostelworld, GetYourGuide, MyProtein, Huel, Gousto and others). If you click these links and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. These links are clearly presented as recommendations. We only recommend products we believe are genuinely useful.

9. Payments

Payment processing is handled by Stripe. We do not store your card details — all payment information is handled securely by Stripe. Stripe's privacy policy can be found at stripe.com/gb/privacy.

10. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal data to any third parties. We only share data with the service providers listed above (Supabase, Anthropic, Google, Stripe) strictly as necessary to operate the service.

11. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — ask us to delete your account and all associated data
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Restriction — ask us to limit how we process your data
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@archie.app. We will respond within 30 days.

12. Data Retention

We retain your account and plan data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all your personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days. Anonymised, aggregated usage statistics may be retained indefinitely.

13. Children's Privacy

Archie is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of Archie after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

15. Complaints

If you have concerns about how we handle your data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK's data protection authority:
